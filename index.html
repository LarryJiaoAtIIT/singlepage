Introduction to Source Filmmaker

Source Film maker is a video-capturing and editing application that works inside the Source Game Engine. The tool, created by ValVe Corporation, has been used to create over 50 animated shorts for its Source games, including Team Fortress 2, the Left 4 Dead series, and Half-Life series.

The Clip Editor

The Clip Editor is used in the final production of your animation. You will be able to see your creation come to life after adding the lights, camera, and the action. Everything piece of effort you have placed will come into full effect with the clip editor, as you place audio clips within the same codecs in here.

The Motion Editor

The Motion Editor is one of the two ways you can animate. Many animators primarily use this to pose different characters.

The Graph Editor

The Graph Editor is the second of the two ways you can animate. With the graph editor, you can provide very granular changes for the inbetweens, adjust the amount of velocity a movement is positioned, as well as change facial structure of the individual playermodels within a keyframe.
